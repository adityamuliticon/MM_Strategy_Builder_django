Strategy Name
•
 Define your strategy name. Ex. Stock PMS1 5Lac, Future PMS1 3Lac
Capital
•
 Total portfolio capital. capital will use to calculate trade qty dynamically.
Trading Type
•
 There are 2 options Intraday & Positional. If you select the Intraday option then the system will close your position at the given exit time.
•
Kindly set exit time from other settings 
Product
•
 There are 3 options for product 1. MIS 2. NRML 3. CNC, System will place an order as per your selection.
•
If you select MIS product type then the broker(RMS) will close your order automatically if you do not close from our system. 
Master Target
•
 You can set a master target by Money or capital (%).
The system will close all open positions if MTM touches the master target.
•
Once your master target gets hit after that new position will not take until the strategy gets reset. 
Master SL
•
 You can set a master sl by Money or capital (%).
The system will close all open positions if MTM touches the master sl.
•
Once your master sl gets hit after that new position will not take until the strategy gets reset. 
Max Position
•
 The system will not take a new position if you take more than the defined max position.
Ex. No of symbols is 10 and Max Positon=8 then the system will take a maximum of 8 symbol positions only. It will reject the call if you try to take the 9th position
•
Set max position = 0 to avoid this condition 
Max Capital Allocation(%)
•
 Restrict symbol-wise max capital utilization.
Fox ex. if we have defined max capital allocation as 10% for each symbol and a particular symbol has 20% then it will calculate qty max up to 10% for each symbol.
•
This condition will activate if any symbol has a qty distribution type is a capital risk(%) 
Qty Distribution
•
 There are 4 types of qty distribution methods.

1. Fix -> Define fix qty for a single trade. 
For Ex Qty = 5

2. Capital(%) -> System will calculate qty based on available capital.
For Ex. Available Capital = 5,00,000/- & Capital(%) = 2
Qty = (Available Capital * 2/100) / stock price

3. Capital Risk(%) -> System will calculate qty based on available capital & symbol max stop loss(SL).
For Ex. Available Capital=5,00,000/- Capital Risk(%)=20% & Symbol SL is 1000
Risk Capital = 5,00,000*20/100 = 1,00,000
Qty = Risk Capital/SL

4. Allocation Method 1 -> System will calculate qty based on available no of position & available capital. 
Fox Ex. Total Capital=5,00,000/- Available Cpital=2,00,000/- Available Position=5
Free Margin for symbol = 2,00,000/5 = 40,000/-
Qty = 40,000/stock price
